About

Welcome to The Fig & The Olive

The Fig & The Olive is a Halal eatery offering a diverse menu that caters to various tastes, with a particular emphasis on evening dining. The restaurant features an array of pizzas with unique names like F&O Chefs Special and Naples, alongside hearty hot sandwiches including a Steak Melt and Tuna Melt. Popular items among patrons include the Chicken Club, The Crispy Chicken, and Samosas. For those with a sweet tooth, desserts such as Sticky Toffee Pudding and Cookie Dough are available. Mozzarella Sticks are frequently ordered together, highlighting their appeal as a favourite snack.
